The Challenge

Before this portal existed…

❌ No Central Tracking

Branding requests handled through emails. No single source of truth for what's branded, pending, or failed.

❌ No Photo Verification

No formal process to confirm branding was done correctly. Suppliers had no way to prove their work.

❌ Manual Coordination

Scheduling suppliers, assigning slots, chasing logistics partners for details — all done manually.

❌ Zero Fleet Visibility

Snoonu had no real-time view of branded vehicles, their companies, or currently assigned drivers.

🔄 3PL — Tab 5

Driver Replacement

Reassign a branded vehicle to a new driver — without triggering a full re-branding cycle.

1

Select vehicle + new driver

Pick from branded vehicles dropdown. New driver auto-fetched & validated as same company.

2

Submit for Admin review

Admin sees old driver vs new driver side-by-side — approves or rejects.

✓

Record updated instantly

Branded Vehicles shows new driver. Branding status unchanged.

💡 Why This Exists

Drivers change vehicles frequently. Without this, a 3PL would need a full sticker removal + re-branding just to update a driver name — wasting time and supplier cost. Replacement Requests solve it in one step.

🛡️ Guardrail

New driver must belong to the same 3PL company. Any other company's driver ID is automatically rejected.

🔧 Role 3 — Supplier

The Branding Contractor

Suppliers execute branding jobs on the ground. Their portal is lean and focused — they only see jobs assigned to them, upload photo proof on-site, and track their completed work for payment.

Tab 1 — Assigned Schedule

All jobs assigned to them — plate, driver, date, time, area. Two actions per job:
Upload 3 Photos when done, or Did Not Appear if driver is absent.

📸 Camera-Only

Photos captured directly from the device camera. No file browsing — ensures fresh, on-site evidence every time.

Tab 2 — Sticker Removed

Full history of all sticker removal jobs completed. Plate, company, driver, date. Used as the reference for payment reconciliation — each record = one billable work order.

Tab 3 — Branded Vehicles

Full history of all branding and re-branding jobs completed. Same purpose as Sticker Removed — the supplier's completed jobs ledger for billing and payment tracking.

Workflow A

New Branding

The most common flow. A vehicle goes from unbranded to verified in the registry.

1

3PL submits New Branding Request

Driver, plate, vehicle details entered. Duplicate plate check runs automatically.

2

Admin assigns Supplier + Slot

Picks from accordion supplier list. Slot capacity decreases by 1.

3

Supplier applies branding + uploads 3 photos

Camera-captured photos uploaded from the job site.

4

Admin reviews and approves photos

Rejects with note if any issue — supplier re-uploads.

✓

Vehicle added to Branded Vehicles

Visible to the 3PL immediately. Job marked Completed.

Status Journey
Pending — Awaiting admin
Scheduled — Supplier assigned
Awaiting Verification — Photos uploaded
Completed — Admin approved ✅
⏱️ Typical Timeline

From submission to completion: 1–3 days depending on supplier availability and admin verification speed. All parties have live visibility throughout.

Workflow B

Sticker Removal & Re-branding

When a sticker needs replacing — two-phase, largely automated.

1

3PL submits Sticker Removal Request

Only plates already in Branded Vehicles are valid.

2

Admin assigns Supplier for removal

Same slot assignment process as Workflow A.

3

Supplier removes sticker + uploads 3 photos

Camera photos from job site as evidence.

⚡

Admin verifies → Re-branding job auto-created

Vehicle logged in Sticker Removed. System automatically creates a Re-branding job — no manual step.

5

Re-branding job assigned → executed → verified

Identical flow to Workflow A from this point.

✓

Vehicle back in Branded Vehicles

New branding date and supplier recorded.

⚡ The Key Automation

The moment Admin approves the sticker removal, the system automatically creates the Re-branding job. Admin doesn't open a new request — they simply see the new job appear in their schedule, ready to assign.

One Request, Two Jobs

The 3PL submits one request. The system internally handles two separate jobs — ensuring nothing is missed between phases. Both are separately recorded with full audit trails.

System Intelligence

Built-in Guardrails

Rules enforced automatically — no one can accidentally bypass the workflow or corrupt data.

🏢

Own-Company Drivers Only

A 3PL can only submit requests for drivers from their own company. Cross-company driver IDs are blocked with an error message.

🔎

Duplicate Plate Detection

If a plate is already in Branded Vehicles, the system catches it on entry and displays the existing driver, company, and vehicle — submission is blocked.

📸

All 3 Photos Required

Suppliers cannot submit a job for verification unless all three photos are captured. The button is disabled until all three are present.

🛡️

Admin Verification is Mandatory

A vehicle cannot be marked Branded by a supplier alone. Admin approval of photo evidence is the only path to completion — the step cannot be skipped.

📅

Slot Capacity Enforcement

Supplier slots have a maximum capacity. Full slots are greyed out automatically — overbooking is impossible.
